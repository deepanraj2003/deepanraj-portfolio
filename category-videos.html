VIDEO EDITING

DARK GAMING & LIVE STREAMING

DARK is a high-energy gaming and live streaming channel focused on competitive play.

THE EDITING STRATEGY

For a live-streaming brand, the video edits needed to capture the most intense moments without losing the creator's personality. We focused on beat-syncing gameplay to high-energy tracks and implementing tactical motion graphics that match the "Dark" aesthetic.

TECHNICAL EXECUTION

We utilized advanced transitions and speed ramping to maintain a relentless pace that keeps viewers engaged throughout the entire duration. These highlights serve as powerful promotional content for the channel's live sessions.

THE MONTAGE MASTERCLASS
