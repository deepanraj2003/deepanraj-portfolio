LOGO DESIGNING

DRJ PHOTOGRAPHY

A professional photography brand focused on capturing high-fidelity imagery.

THE DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

The identity for DRJ Photography was built around the concept of a "seal of quality". Using the DRJPHOTO.jpg asset, we designed a circular badge that mirrors the lens of a camera, creating a symmetrical and professional watermark.

DARK GAMING IDENTITY

A high-intensity gaming channel dedicated to competitive play.

AVATAR & PERSONA BRANDING

For the DARK.jpg identity, the goal was to create a digital persona that resonates with the gaming community. We chose a tactical mascot design featuring a mysterious, masked figure to evoke a sense of focus and elite skill.

DRJ OFFICIALS HUB

The main administrative and content hub.

THE PRESTIGE IDENTITY

Utilizing the DRJ.jpg asset, we developed a logo that emphasizes legacy and achievement. The use of the laurel wreath and triple-star motifs is a strategic choice to project authority and a "gold standard" in content production.

DARK BEATS & AUDIO

A lifestyle and music-focused channel.

AUDIO-VISUAL SYNERGY

The DARK BEATS.jpg logo was designed to immediately communicate the channel's musical focus. By utilizing a stylized headset icon with integrated "D" branding, we created a minimalist and modern look.
