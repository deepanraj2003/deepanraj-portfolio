BANNER DESIGNING

DARK GAMING & LIVE STREAMING

DARK is a high-energy gaming and live streaming channel that focuses on real-time audience engagement and competitive play.

V1: THE FOUNDATION (BLUE ERA)

When the client first launched, they requested a clean, professional blue-themed identity to establish trust and clarity. We achieved a balanced layout that prioritized readability and satisfied the initial launch requirements.

V2: THE AGGRESSIVE PIVOT (RED ERA)

After months of growth, the client felt the blue was too calm for their gameplay. They wanted a more "darkish" and aggressive palette. We transitioned to high-contrast Red and Black to reflect a competitive edge.

V3: THE ROYAL PEAK (GOLD ERA)

For the final evolution, the client wanted to move into a "Prestige" category. We achieved this by implementing the Royal Gold theme. This design used cinematic lighting and deep shadows to mark the peak of the channel's branding.

DRJ CREATIONZ

DRJ Creationz is a versatile content creation channel specializing in short-form videos.

FESTIVE STRATEGY

Since this project was commissioned during the Christmas season, we added snowflake effects and a star-field background. The client loved how these touches added warmth without compromising the high-end feel.

DRJ GAMING

DRJ Gaming is a specialized gaming montage channel.

MONTAGE MASTERY
